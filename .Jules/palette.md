## 2026-05-30 - Inspinia Template Accessibility
**Learning:** Inspinia-based templates in this repository often have a recurring pattern where icon-only buttons (toggles, dropdowns) lack 'aria-label' attributes and font-awesome classes occasionally contain 'clfa' typos instead of 'fa'.
**Action:** Always check for 'aria-label' on icon-only buttons and search for 'clfa' typos when working with header or navigation templates.
