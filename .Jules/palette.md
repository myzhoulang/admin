## 2025-05-22 - 404 Page Recovery Path
**Learning:** A 404 page without a functional search or clear navigation acts as a dead-end, increasing user frustration. Restoring search functionality and fixing typos significantly improves the "recovery" experience.
**Action:** Always ensure 404 pages provide at least one functional way to return to a known state (search, home link) and maintain the same level of polish as the rest of the app.
