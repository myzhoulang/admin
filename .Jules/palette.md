## 2025-05-14 - Standardizing Form Accessibility
**Learning:** Found that the Forget Password form lacks proper labels and keyboard submission support. Using `.sr-only` labels and `ng-submit` improves accessibility without altering the visual design of the Inspinia theme.
**Action:** Apply `sr-only` labels, `ng-submit`, and the AngularJS "dot rule" (`$scope.f`) to the Forget Password form.
